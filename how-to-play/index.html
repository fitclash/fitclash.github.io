    How to Play

    Game Summary
    Moonwalk is a fitness accountability app that boosts daily step goals through financial and social incentives.

    How to Win
    To win the game, keep pace with the highest days completed with others in your crew.

    1.

    INSTALL A STEP PROVIDER

    Download and install a step provider such as Google Fit or Fitbit onto your mobile device. If you use Apple Health, use Google Fit to connect to Apple Health's step data.

    2.

    WALLET CONNECT

    Launch app using a popular defi wallet such as Phantom or Solflare and connect.

    3.

    JOIN A GAME

    Enter the game code of an existing game. To ensure participants have a personal stake and genuine motivation, Moonwalk operates on an invite-only system. This ensures fairness and emphasizes the primary goal of fitness over finance.

    4.

    or CREATE A GAME

    Create a game by setting a daily step target, a duration of days and the token used for the game. Invite friends and family to join in on the fun.

    5.

    HOW TO WIN

    Your goal is to hit the crew's daily step target consistently. Keep pace with the highest days completed by others in the crew to protect your deposit.

    6.

    CLAIM

    When the game ends, claim back your full deposit if you kept pace with the top users in your crew. If there are others who failed to keep pace, you will receive a portion of their deposit as well.
