Fitness Accountability

Add crypto into a pool with your friends. Complete each day of the step challenge and get your deposit back. The total deductions from every day missed by players are split between the winners.

Launch App

Only possible on Solana, OPOS

Moonwalk provides immutable social accountability with a seamless and secure transaction experience by leveraging the power and security of the Solana blockchain to supercharge your existing fitness goals.

Pool your crypto with friends

Engage in fitness challenges with those you know and new friends you will meet.

Set goals - track your steps

Whether solo or in a crew, track your steps using any major fitness application and compete with your crew.

Miss goals - compensate friends

Your deposits are in your control. Players who don't keep pace with the highest days completed will have a portion of their deposits distributed to the winners.

Hit goals - hold your deposits

Hit the crew goal or stay on pace with the highest days completed to hold your deposit and to collect deductions from those who fell below the threshold.

Pool with BONK, SOL, USDC

Games can be played with BONK, SOL or USDC from the Solana blockchain. Participating in games played with BONK may be incentivized.
