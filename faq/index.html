FAQ

General Questions

What is Moonwalk?

Moonwalk is a fitness accountability app that combines daily step goals with financial incentives. Users can join or create a crew, set a daily step target, and stake their crypto like USDC, SOL, or BONK. Achieving goals protects your deposit, while failing leads to a forfeiture of a portion of your stake.

Can I play Moonwalk solo?

Yes, Moonwalk can be played solo for personal accountability, though it is primarily designed for collaborative play.

What are the supported crypto for gameplay?

Users can stake USDC, SOL, or BONK to play Moonwalk games.

Is Moonwalk suitable for beginners in fitness?

Absolutely! Moonwalk is designed to motivate and engage people at all fitness levels. Just ensure you set achievable step goals based on your current fitness level.

Getting Started

How do I join a Moonwalk game?

To join a game, you need to be invited by an existing crew member. You can join via a game invite link or by entering a game code after launching the Moonwalk app and connecting your Solana Wallet.

What is required to create a Moonwalk profile?

You need to connect a Solana Wallet (see Solana General Section) and select a step provider (Google Fit, Apple Health via Google Fit, or Fitbit). You'll also need to provide the email associated with your device for the step provider.

Can I join a game without an invite or game code?

No, you need an invite or game code to participate in games. However, you can still create a Moonwalk profile and wait for an invite.

I'm new to fitness apps. How do I choose a step provider?

Choose a step provider based on the fitness tracking device or app you currently use or plan to use. Google Fit and Apple Health are popular choices and can sync with various fitness devices.

Gameplay

How do I start playing after setting up my profile?

After joining a game and opting into your step provider, you need to deposit the required tokens set by the crew captain. Ensure your step provider app is synced daily with your Moonwalk profile.

What happens if I meet or fail to meet my daily step goal?

If you meet the daily crew step goal or keep pace with the crew leaders, your deposit is safe. If you fail, you forfeit a portion of your deposit, which is distributed among successful crew members.

How are game winnings distributed?

Winnings are distributed based on the number of successful daily targets met relative to other crew members. The total deposit pool is divided proportionally among successful participants.

What if I don't meet my daily step goal?

If you don't meet your goal, a portion of your deposit is forfeited and shared among those who did meet their goals. It's a way to motivate you to stay active, but remember to set realistic goals.

Are there any fees for participating in Moonwalk games?

Moonwalk does not charge any fees on games. All games operate as a closed system for deposits.

Can I change my daily step goal?

The daily step goal is set at the beginning of each game by the crew's captain. It's fixed for the duration of that game, so communicate with your captain if you have concerns about the set goal.

What happens at the end of a game?

At the end of a game, your performance is evaluated based on the step goals. Depending on your success, you can either reclaim your full deposit or receive a portion of it, with the rest distributed to successful crew members.

Solana Introduction

What is a Solana Wallet and why do I need it for Moonwalk?

A Solana Wallet is a digital wallet used to store Solana (SOL) and other cryptocurrencies on the Solana blockchain. In Moonwalk, it's used to hold your stake (deposit) in the game. If you're new to cryptocurrencies, think of it as a special account for digital money.

How do I set up a Solana Wallet?

To set up a Solana Wallet, you can download a wallet app like Phantom or Solflare. Follow the app's instructions to create a new wallet. Remember to keep your wallet's recovery phrase safe and private.

What are USDC, SOL, and BONK?

USDC: A stablecoin cryptocurrency whose value is pegged to the US dollar.
SOL: The native cryptocurrency of the Solana blockchain.
BONK: A social token deeply rooted in the Solana Community.

How do I get cryptocurrencies like USDC, SOL, or BONK?

You can buy these cryptocurrencies on various exchanges. You'll need to create an account on an exchange, purchase the cryptocurrency, and then transfer it to your Solana Wallet.

Technical and Privacy

How do I ensure my steps are counted accurately?

Carry your connected fitness device (like a smartphone or fitness tracker) with you throughout the day. Open your fitness application regularly for Moonwalk to sync your step count.

How does Moonwalk ensure the privacy of my data?

Moonwalk takes user privacy seriously. For detailed information on data handling and privacy, visit moonwalk.fit/terms-of-service, moonwalk.fit/privacy-policy and moonwalk.fit/cookie-policy.

What happens if I have technical issues with the app?

For technical support, you can contact Moonwalk's customer service through the app or visit the support section on the Moonwalk website.

Miscellaneous

Can I play Moonwalk with friends in different time zones?

Yes, the game accommodates different time zones by starting and ending each user's day at 00:00 in their respective time zones.

Is Moonwalk available on all mobile platforms?

Moonwalk supports popular step providers like Google Fit, Apple Health, and Fitbit. Ensure your device is compatible with these services.

How often do I need to sync my step provider with Moonwalk?

You should open your selected step provider fitness app daily to sync your steps with your Moonwalk profile for accurate goal progression tracking. Google Fit is the only step provider that requires manual opening of the app for steps to sync.
